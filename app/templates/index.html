Rigatoni

Rigatoni, which comes from the word “rigato” meaning ridged, are short, wide tubes of pasta that have ridges on the outside, but are smooth on the inside. Rigatoni are perfect for big chunkier sauces as their ridges and wide center help capture the sauce perfectly.

Campanelle

Campanelle has a gentle but sturdy shape, with an Italian name that translates to “bellflowers” in English. The pasta has thin, fluted, petal-like edges and a hollow center which is perfect for capturing sauce. It also goes well with meat sauces, fish-based sauces, or robust tomato-based sauces.
